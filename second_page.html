Things to do

🌴 Outdoor & Beach Activities South Beach – Iconic for its Art Deco architecture and vibrant beach scene. Wynwood Walls – An open-air street art museum with massive murals by artists from around the world. Bayside Marketplace – Waterfront shopping and dining with live music and boat tours. Vizcaya Museum & Gardens – A beautiful Italian-style villa and gardens.

🌊 On the Water Boat tours of Biscayne Bay – See celebrity homes on Star Island. Jet skiing or paddleboarding – Especially around Key Biscayne or Miami Beach. Snorkeling or diving – Head down to nearby Key Largo for clear waters and coral reefs.

🌆 Nightlife & Culture Little Havana – Experience Cuban culture, cigars, live music, and authentic food. Live music and clubs – Check out E11EVEN or LIV at the Fontainebleau. Perez Art Museum Miami (PAMM) – Modern art with views of the bay.

🍽️ Food & Drink Try stone crab (seasonal) at Joe’s Stone Crab. Hit up Versailles in Little Havana for Cuban cuisine. Explore the Design District for trendy dining and high-end shopping.
